Frequently asked questions and troubleshooting guides

📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

Click on "Add Product" in the navigation menu, fill in the product details (barcode, name, category, price, stock), and click "Add Product". The product will appear in your inventory immediately.

How do I process a sale?

1. Browse products from the Products page
2. Click on any product to add it to cart
3. Adjust quantities in the cart as needed
4. Apply any discount coupons
5. Select payment method
6. Click "Checkout" to complete the sale

How do I apply a coupon?

In the cart page, select a coupon from the dropdown menu and click "Apply". The discount will be calculated automatically based on your subtotal.

What happens when stock runs low?

Products with less than 10 units in stock will show a warning icon. You'll also see low stock alerts on the dashboard and product list.

Can I edit or delete a product?

Yes! Go to the Products page, click the "Edit" button next to any product to modify its details, or "Delete" to remove it from inventory.

How do I search for a product?

Use the Search page. You can search by product name, barcode, or category. The search is case-insensitive and shows real-time results.

🔧 Troubleshooting Guide

Product won't add to cart

Check if the product is in stock. If stock is 0, you cannot add it to cart. Restock the product first by editing it.

Coupon not applying

Ensure your cart subtotal meets the minimum requirement ($100 for $10 off, $200 for $20 off). Percentage coupons have no minimum.

Page not loading properly

Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.
